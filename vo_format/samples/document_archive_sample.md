# Mariana Deep — Station Log Archive

Document Reference: MD-001
Subject: Descent Protocol, Revision 3

The descent begins at the surface tender and proceeds in staged pressure
locks. Each crew member confirms seal integrity before the next gate opens.
No gate opens until the previous one reads green.

[Sound of a pressure door cycling]

Document Reference: MD-002
Subject: Bioluminescence Survey, Sector 7

Sector 7 returned the brightest readings on record. The survey drone logged
three unclassified organisms near the thermal vent before the current
pulled it off station. No samples were recovered.

Document Reference: MD-003
Subject: Incident Report — Lost Contact

Contact with the lower array dropped at 03:14. Telemetry kept arriving for
six minutes after the crew stopped answering the hail. The last packet
logged a depth of 4,110 metres and a falling hull temperature. After that,
nothing.
