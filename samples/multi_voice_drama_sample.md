# The Long Watch — Scene 1

**CAPTAIN VEX:**
Report. And keep it short — we lost the buffer an hour ago.

RILO:
Short version, we're drifting. Long version, I'll need a drink first.

[a low alarm chirps twice, then stops]

**CAPTAIN VEX:**
That's the third time tonight. Mute it.

RILO:
(muttering)
Can't mute what isn't wired to anything.

NARRATOR:
Rilo popped the panel anyway. The wiring behind it had been cut clean, and
not recently.
